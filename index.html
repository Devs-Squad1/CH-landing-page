COMING SOON

As the world fell deeper into isolation, a group of elite moguls gathered. They vowed to never let the party stop, and moved it into the metaverse in an effort to transcend time and space.

Thus, Cyberhaus was born: a collective aimed at keeping the music going by constantly hosting events featuring new talents and building a unique Ethos.
